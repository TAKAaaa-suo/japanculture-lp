Pokémon Items

1. Pokémon Center Mega Tokyo (Sunshine Ikebukuro)

Permanent store inside Sunshine. Many items are “this store only”—top priority for Pokémon hunters.

Many fans visit Japan specifically for “cafe-only” goods. Reservation required in most cases.

Cafe-limited rubber coaster / rubber goods

With visit/order or at goods counter (coaster often as order/reservation bonus). Reprint: medium–low.

Sold at cafe only. Reprint: depends on store (while stock lasts).
