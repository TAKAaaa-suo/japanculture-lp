Welcome to JapanCulture. By using our service, you agree to the following Terms of Service. Please read them carefully before submitting a request.

1. Service Description

JapanCulture operates a proxy purchasing service (also referred to as "buying agent" or "purchasing agent" service). We purchase items from Japanese retail stores on your behalf and ship them to your address internationally.

Important: JapanCulture is not an e-commerce store and does not sell merchandise. We do not hold inventory. Every purchase is made at a physical retail store in Japan based on your specific request. This is a service (task-based engagement), not a product sale.

When you place a request, you are commissioning us to visit a specific store, locate and purchase the requested item(s), verify authenticity and condition, and arrange international shipping to you. Ownership of the purchased item(s) transfers to you at the point of purchase at the store.

2. Eligibility

Our service is currently available to customers in the following countries only:

  • Singapore (SG)
  • United States (US)
  • Australia (AU)

You must be at least 18 years of age to use this service. By submitting a request, you confirm that you meet these eligibility requirements. Requests from countries outside the above list will not be accepted.

3. How It Works

The proxy purchasing process follows these steps:

  1. Request — You submit a request form specifying the item(s) you want, preferred store, reference photos, and budget limit.
  2. Quote — We send you a detailed quote within 10 minutes, including: service fee, commission, estimated item cost, shipping estimate, and packaging fee (all in USD).
  3. Payment — You review and pay the quoted amount. No purchase is made until payment is received.
  4. Purchase — On the scheduled Store Run day (every Friday), we visit the designated store and purchase your item(s).
  5. Verification — We inspect the item(s), photograph them for confirmation, and store them at our facility.
  6. Shipping — Items are shipped internationally on fixed shipping dates (twice per month) with tracking provided.

If the requested item is unavailable at the designated store, we will attempt to locate it at nearby stores. If we cannot purchase it, you will be notified and offered a full refund or store credit.

4. Pricing

All prices are quoted and charged in US Dollars (USD). Our fee structure consists of a service fee plus a commission based on the item price:

Service Type Service Fee Commission
Standard (1-2 items) $45 15% of item price
Standard (3+ items) $45 12% of item price
Rare Item Search $65 18% of item price
Event Proxy (per event) $80 20% of item price

Additional costs borne by the customer include: shipping, packaging ($2-$6 depending on size), and any applicable customs duties or import taxes.

The exchange rate between JPY and USD is locked at the time the quote is issued. The quoted amount will not change due to subsequent exchange rate fluctuations.

For the complete fee breakdown and calculation examples, please see our Fees page.

5. Payment

All payments must be made in advance. We will not purchase any item until full payment has been received. Accepted payment methods:

  • PayPal
  • Stripe (credit/debit card)
  • Wise (bank transfer)

Quotes are valid for 24 hours from the time of issuance. If payment is not received within this period, the quote will expire and a new quote may need to be requested.

JapanCulture does not store your credit card information. All payment processing is handled securely by the respective payment provider (PayPal, Stripe, or Wise).

6. Cancellation & Refund

Before Purchase

If we have not yet purchased the item at the store, you may cancel your request and receive a full refund, no questions asked.

After Purchase

Once we have purchased the item at the store, cash refunds are not available. Instead, we will issue a store credit for the full amount paid. Since the item has already been purchased on your behalf and ownership has transferred to you, we cannot reverse the transaction.

Store credit terms:

  • Valid for 90 days from the date of issuance
  • Partial use is allowed — you may split the credit across multiple future requests
  • Expired credit cannot be carried over or extended

For details on store credit, see Section 11.

8. Customs & Import Duties

All customs duties, import taxes, and any fees imposed by your country's customs authority are entirely the customer's responsibility. JapanCulture has no control over these charges and cannot predict the exact amounts.

We will include an estimated customs/duty amount in your quote where possible, but the final amount is determined by your country's customs authority upon arrival.

If your package is held or returned by customs, any additional fees or re-shipping costs are the customer's responsibility.

Notice for United States Customers

Please be aware that the U.S. government has revised the de minimis exemption (previously $800 threshold for duty-free imports). Depending on the current regulations at the time of your import, duties may apply to packages that were previously exempt. We recommend checking the latest U.S. Customs and Border Protection guidelines before placing your order.

9. Liability

Shipping Damage or Loss

In the event of damage or loss during transit, JapanCulture will assist with the claim process within the scope of the carrier's shipping insurance. Our liability is limited to the coverage provided by the shipping carrier (EMS, DHL, or FedEx). We recommend reviewing the carrier's insurance terms for high-value items.

Item Unavailability

If an item is out of stock at the designated store and we cannot locate it at alternative stores, we will promptly notify you and offer your choice of:

  • A full refund of the amount paid for that item, or
  • Store credit of the equivalent amount

Authenticity Guarantee

We purchase exclusively from official, authorized Japanese retail stores. We do not handle counterfeit, replica, or bootleg merchandise. Every item is photographed after purchase for your verification before shipping.

Limitation

JapanCulture is not responsible for delays caused by customs processing, postal service disruptions, natural disasters, or other force majeure events. Our total liability for any single request shall not exceed the total amount paid by the customer for that request.
