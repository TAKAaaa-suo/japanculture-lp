Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

JapanCulture ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our proxy purchasing service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information when you submit a purchase request or create an order:

  • Full name — to identify your order and label shipments
  • Shipping address — to deliver purchased items to you
  • Email address — to send quotes, order updates, and shipping notifications
  • Phone number (optional) — to contact you about urgent order issues
  • Item request details — product names, descriptions, reference images, and budget limits you provide
  • Payment information — processed securely through Wise, PayPal, or Stripe. We do not store your credit card numbers, bank account details, or other financial credentials on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Preparing and sending quotes for requested items
  • Executing proxy purchases on your behalf at Japanese retail stores
  • Shipping purchased items to your designated address
  • Communicating with you regarding your orders, including confirmations, updates, and delivery tracking
  • Improving our service quality, store route planning, and overall customer experience

3. Third-Party Sharing

We share your personal information only with the following third parties, strictly for the purposes of fulfilling your order:

  • Wise / PayPal / Stripe — for secure payment processing
  • Japan Post / DHL / FedEx — for international shipping and delivery
  • Tally — for processing request forms submitted on our website
  • Airtable — for internal order management and tracking

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Retention

We retain your order data (including name, shipping address, email, and order history) for two (2) years after the completion of your transaction. This retention period allows us to handle any post-delivery inquiries, returns, or disputes.

After the two-year retention period, your personal data is permanently deleted from our systems. You may also request earlier deletion at any time (see "Your Rights" below).

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies solely through Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors interact with our site. Google Analytics collects:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or source
  • Browser type, device type, and screen resolution
  • Approximate geographic location (country/city level)
  • Anonymized IP addresses

Google Analytics data is used exclusively to improve our website and service. We do not use advertising cookies or any other third-party tracking tools. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — You may request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion — You may request that we delete your personal data from our systems.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@japanculture.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

7. Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement the following measures:

  • SSL/TLS encryption — All data transmitted between your browser and our website is encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols.
  • Third-party payment processing — Payment information is handled entirely by Wise, PayPal, and Stripe, all of which are PCI DSS compliant. We never have access to your full card or bank details.
  • Access controls — Only authorized team members can access order data, and access is limited to what is necessary to fulfill your order.

While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children

Our service is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that we have collected data from a person under 18, we will take steps to delete that information promptly. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us at hello@japanculture.com.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: hello@japanculture.com
