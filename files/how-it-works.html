How it works

From request to delivery: check schedule → send request → get quote → pay → we buy & store → we ship & you track.

1. Check schedule

On the homepage you’ll see the next store visit date, order deadline (Thursday), and which stores we’re visiting. Place your request before the deadline.

2. Send request

Use the request form to send: product name, reference photo (optional), preferred store or “any”, and budget if you like. From an article you can pre-fill the item.

3. Get quote

We’ll email you a quote within 24 hours with: request fee, item fee, shipping, packaging, and total (all in USD). Quote is valid for 24 hours.

4. Pay

Pay via the link we send. We do not buy until payment is complete. No surprises—shipping is confirmed in the quote before you pay.

5. We buy & store

On the scheduled Friday we visit the store(s) and buy your items. We check, photograph if needed, and store for up to 30 days so we can combine shipments.

6. We ship & you track

We ship twice per month. You’ll get a tracking number by email. We ship to Singapore, United States, and Australia. Customs and duties are your responsibility.
