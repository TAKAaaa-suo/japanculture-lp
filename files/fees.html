Standard proxy (store items)

Examples: anime goods from Animate, Toranoana, general store stock.

Item Amount (USD)
Request fee (per request) $45
Item fee (1–2 items) 15% of item total
Item fee (3+ items) 12% of item total

Example: 2 items totaling $100 → $45 + $15 = $60 (fees only; plus shipping & packaging).

Rare / secondhand / specialty

For hard-to-find items. Examples: out-of-print figures, specific doujinshi, dolls, discontinued cards. We search multiple stores; if we can't find it, you pay only $20 research fee.

Item Amount (USD)
Request fee (per request) $65
Item fee (when found) 18% of item total
If not found $20 research fee only

We’ll confirm this option and the $20 research fee before you commit.
