Frequently Asked Questions

What if the item is sold out?

If we can't purchase your requested item, we'll try other nearby stores first. If it's truly unavailable, you choose: full refund or store credit (valid 90 days). You're never stuck paying for something we couldn't get.

How long does shipping take?

We ship twice per month on fixed dates. Delivery takes 7-14 days depending on your country and shipping method (EMS/DHL/FedEx). You'll receive a tracking number as soon as the package is shipped. Use our free 30-day storage to combine orders and save on shipping.

Can I cancel after payment?

Before we purchase: Full refund, no questions asked.

After we purchase at the store: We issue store credit (valid 90 days, transferable). Since we already own the item at that point, cash refunds are not available after purchase.

How much does shipping cost?

Shipping varies by destination and package size:

  • Singapore: $18 - $45
  • USA: $22 - $55
  • Australia: $24 - $58

You'll see the exact shipping cost in your quote before paying. Combine multiple orders with our free 30-day storage to save up to 66% on shipping.
